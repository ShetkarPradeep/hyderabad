HYDERABAD

Hyderabad, also known as the "City of Pearls", is the capital city of the Indian state of Telangana. It is famous for its rich history, culture, and cuisine.

The city is home to iconic landmarks such as the Charminar, Golconda Fort, and Hussain Sagar Lake.

Hyderabad is a major center for the technology industry, with many multinational IT companies having their offices here, earning it the nickname "Cyberabad".

The cuisine of Hyderabad is renowned worldwide, especially for its biryani and kebabs.

With a blend of tradition and modernity, Hyderabad offers a unique experience to visitors.
